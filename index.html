The System

Three steps, repeated every week

Every piece of content follows the same cycle. This is the operating rhythm for all six weeks, starting with the first 11 videos and designed to scale as new content is produced.

1
Publish
Content goes live on WSB's channels on a set schedule, across the right platforms for each piece.
➜
2
Amplify
Agents receive ready-to-share kits and post to their own networks, turning one post into thousands.
➜
3
Measure
Track performance across every platform. Optimize what's working. Report weekly and adjust.

Between video drops, supporting content keeps the audience engaged: photo carousels, quote cards, Stories, and polls. Think of these as the connective tissue that keeps the conversation going between each major video release.

Where It Goes

Four platforms, each with a role

Not every video goes everywhere. Each platform reaches a different audience and serves a different purpose in the recruiting funnel.

Instagram All 11 videos
Primary hub

Every video publishes here. Reels, Stories, carousels, and polls. Strongest platform for younger demographics and driving saves/shares.

TikTok NEW CHANNEL 10 videos
Discovery engine

A new channel for WSB. TikTok's algorithm-driven distribution means even brand new accounts can achieve significant reach from day one. This is where WSB reaches people who've never heard of it.

LinkedIn 6 videos
Professional credibility

Reaches career-minded professionals considering a move. Financial education and life-stage content perform strongest here.

YouTube Shorts 7 videos
Search + longevity

YouTube content stays discoverable far longer than other platforms. Shorts feed into WSB's broader YouTube presence and surface in Google search.

Measuring What Matters

Every view connects back to recruitment

Content without measurement is guesswork. This framework connects every view and share back to WSB's core goal: bringing in new agents. Here's the conversion path and what gets tracked at each stage.

Awareness
Content reaches new audiences
Interest

Consideration

Recruitment
Prospect becomes an agent
Awareness
Reach & Views

Total views, unique reach, impressions. Split by organic vs. agent-amplified.

Interest
Engagement & Saves

Likes, comments, shares, saves. Save rate is the strongest signal of content value.

Consideration
Clicks & Page Visits

Link clicks from bio, Stories, DMs. Landing page visits. Info session registrations.

Recruitment
Applications

New agent sign-ups attributed to content and to individual referring agents.
