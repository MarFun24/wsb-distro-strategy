Executive Summary

The plan in 60 seconds

WSB has 11 short-form videos ready to go, 200+ event photos, and 35,000 agents who can share that content to their own networks. This plan puts a system around all of it.

Six weeks of structured content
1
Videos go out on a set schedule
Two videos per week across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, and YouTube Shorts, with supporting content (carousels, Stories, polls) filling in between.
2
Agents get ready-to-share kits
Each kit includes the video link, pre-written captions, photos, and posting instructions. Copy, paste, publish. Takes under a minute.
3
Everything is tracked back to recruitment
Every view, click, and sign-up is measured. Weekly reports show what's working, what to adjust, and which agents are driving results.

●
Brand visibility across four platforms, reaching audiences who have never heard of WSB through both official channels and agent networks
●
Agent engagement with a leaderboard, recognition, and content previews that give agents ownership of the campaign
●
A clear path to recruitment from content view to landing page visit to application, with tracking at every step
●
A repeatable system that scales beyond these first 11 videos as new content is produced

The System

Three steps, repeated every week

Every piece of content follows the same cycle. This is the operating rhythm for all six weeks, starting with the first 11 videos and designed to scale as new content is produced.

1
Publish
Content goes live on WSB's channels on a set schedule, across the right platforms for each piece.
➜
2
Amplify
Agents receive ready-to-share kits and post to their own networks, turning one post into thousands.
➜
3
Measure
Track performance across every platform. Optimize what's working. Report weekly and adjust.

Between video drops, supporting content keeps the audience engaged: photo carousels, quote cards, Stories, and polls. Think of these as the connective tissue that keeps the conversation going between each major video release.

Where It Goes

Four platforms, each with a role

Not every video goes everywhere. Each platform reaches a different audience and serves a different purpose in the recruiting funnel.

InstagramAll 11 videos
Primary hub

Every video publishes here. Reels, Stories, carousels, and polls. Strongest platform for younger demographics and driving saves/shares.

TikTok NEW CHANNEL 10 videos
Discovery engine

A new channel for WSB. TikTok's algorithm-driven distribution means even brand new accounts can achieve significant reach from day one. This is where WSB reaches people who've never heard of it.

LinkedIn6 videos
Professional credibility

Reaches career-minded professionals considering a move. Financial education and life-stage content perform strongest here.

YouTube Shorts7 videos
Search + longevity

YouTube content stays discoverable far longer than other platforms. Shorts feed into WSB's broader YouTube presence and surface in Google search.

Next Steps

Four steps to launch

Review and lock the calendar

Set up internal channels

Week 1 goes live

Content publishes. Agent kits go out same day. Tracking starts immediately.
